Burritos

Burritos are an extremely popular Mexican food and have evolved many different styles over the years. Some of which you can learn about below!

Wet Burrito

This style burrito is eaten with a knife and fork, and covered with melted cheese and smotherd in gravy.

California Burrito

Originally from San Diego, this burrito is usually stuffed with carne asada, sour cream, cheese, salsa, guacamole and french fries.

Breakfast Burrito

Made with different combinations of breakfast foods such as eggs, potatoes and sausage, the breakfast burrito provides an early morning burrito opportunity.

Chimichanga

The chimichanga is a type of deep fried burrito originating from Arizona.
